# Terra Agraria: From Soil to Stars (v2.0.1)

A long-term Minecraft 1.21.1 NeoForge modpack built around TerraFirmaCraft,
with Stardew Valley-style farming, cooking, and seasonal gameplay.

## Quick Start
Allocate 6GB+ RAM (6144 MB).

## Stardew Valley-Style Features

### 4 Seasons & Weather
- **Seasonal Let's Do** — 4-season cycle affecting Let's Do mods' crops
- **TFC Seasons** — TFC's own 12-month calendar with spring/summer/autumn/winter

### Massive Crop Variety (100+ crops)
- **Pam's HarvestCraft 2: Food Core** — 80+ crops + 100+ food items
- **Pam's HarvestCraft 2: Food Extended** — even more foods
- **Pam's HarvestCraft 2: Trees** — fruit/nut trees (apple, cherry, almond, etc.)
- **Bountiful Fares** — new crops, fruits, and forageables
- **Croptopia Delight** — Croptopia crops + Farmer's Delight cooking
- **Expanded Delight** — extra Farmer's Delight content
- **Mystical Agriculture** — grow resources (iron, gold, diamonds) as crops!

### Deep Cooking (Stardew-style kitchen)
- **Farmer's Delight** (core) — cooking pot, meals, sandwiches
- **Cooking for Blockheads** — cooking station + auto-crafting from fridge
- **Cuisine Delight** — extra cooking recipes
- **Miner's Delight** — cave-themed cooking
- **Let's Do: Farm & Charm** — roasting, jam-making, cooking stations
- **Let's Do: Meadow** — dairy, cheese-making, honey
- **Let's Do: Vinery** — wine-making
- **Spice of Life: Onion** — rewards eating varied foods

### Fishing (Stardew-style minigame)
- **Stardew Fishing** — fishing minigame inspired by Stardew Valley
- **Stardew Valley Food** — SV-themed food items

### Farming QoL
- **Universal Bone Meal** — bonemeal works on more plants
- **Harvest with Ease** — right-click to harvest + replant
- **Botany Pots** — grow crops in pots (compact farming)

### Late-Game Tech
- Create, Mekanism, Immersive Engineering, Applied Energistics 2
- Storage Drawers, Tom's Storage, Sophisticated Storage, AE2

## Keybinds
- **I**: Field Guide (TFC manual)
- **Default quest book key**: FTB Quests book

## Tips
- Read the Field Guide! TFC changes almost everything.
- Stockpile food before winter (TFC + Seasonal Let's Do).
- Plant crops in spring for best harvest.
- Use Cooking for Blockheads to auto-craft meals from your storage.
- Try fishing with Stardew Fishing — it's a fun minigame!
- The quest book is your roadmap. Follow chapters in order.

Enjoy the journey from soil to stars — now with Stardew Valley flavor!
